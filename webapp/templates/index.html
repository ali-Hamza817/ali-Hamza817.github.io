Clinical Note

This tool is for research and academic purposes only. All predictions are generated by models trained on publicly available datasets. The F2-optimized framework penalizes False Negatives 4× more than False Positives, aligned with the clinical priority of never missing metastasis. Not for clinical diagnosis.
